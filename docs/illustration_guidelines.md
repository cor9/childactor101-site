# ILLUSTRATION_GUIDELINES.md

Illustrations are one of the defining elements of the Child Actor 101 brand.

Every major lesson should begin with a custom illustration that communicates the emotional theme before the first paragraph is read.

---

# Purpose

Illustrations should tell stories.

Not decorate pages.

Every illustration should answer:

"What emotion should the reader feel before reading this lesson?"

---

# Style

- Pixar-inspired cinematic storytelling
- Warm lighting
- Expressive characters
- Clear emotional moments
- Editorial composition
- Family friendly
- Optimistic
- Authentic

---

# Avoid

- Generic stock AI images
- Visual clutter
- Empty character poses
- Horror or dark themes
- Hyper-realism
- Cheap clip-art aesthetics

---

# Composition

One clear focal point.

Readable emotion.

Natural body language.

Strong lighting.

Simple backgrounds that support the story.

---

# Brand Role

Illustrations are part of the educational experience.

They should make complex ideas feel approachable.
