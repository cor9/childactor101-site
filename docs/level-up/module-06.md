## Source Material Summary

This module is centered on understanding representation from the inside: what agents do, what managers do, and how families can become the kind of clients reps want to advocate for.

### The Agent's Job

Major themes:

- Negotiation
- Career guidance
- Audition opportunities
- Maintaining actor materials
- Professional networking

Key ideas:

- Agents negotiate deals, interpret contract language, and balance financial value with long-term career strategy.
- They help actors secure auditions that actually match the actor's brand and trajectory.
- They guide actors on headshots, resumes, reels, websites, and current materials.
- Their network with casting directors, studios, and other gatekeepers is one of their greatest assets.

### The Manager's Job

Major themes:

- Career guidance
- Making connections
- Aiding in negotiations
- Project selection
- Image and public relations
- Personal support
- Professional development

Key ideas:

- Managers often take a more holistic role than agents.
- They help shape long-term career direction, public image, training choices, and strategic opportunities.
- They collaborate with agents and lawyers, but in California they do not legally procure work or directly negotiate the way agents do.
- They are often deeply involved in project selection, personal support, and the emotional side of the actor's development.

### Brand and Public Image

This section addresses:

- defining the actor's brand
- maintaining consistency across platforms
- aligning project choices with image
- preparing for interviews and public communication
- social media and online presence

### Professional Development

The module also covers:

- identifying strengths and weaknesses
- recommending coaches and workshops
- private audition prep
- ongoing skill development
- self-assessment and performance review
- parental involvement in healthy growth

Overall, this module is strong source material for lessons about rep relationships, professional behavior, client readiness, and the systems that make actors easier to advocate for.
