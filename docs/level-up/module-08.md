## Source Material Summary

This module is built around practical parent operations: systems, organization, materials, and financial habits that keep the career manageable.

### Time-Management Hacks

Core guidance includes:

- building routines
- prioritizing tasks
- using planners
- setting goals
- time blocking
- preparing for auditions in advance
- delegating where possible
- reducing distractions
- protecting breaks, self-care, and family time
- building in buffer time

This section is strong source material for family workflow and parent-operations lessons.

### Slate Shot

This section explains the Actors Access slate shot as a short impression-driven video asset.

Core ideas:

- use charisma
- stand out with personality
- make a quick memorable impression

### The Slate

This section covers self-tape slates.

Key guidance:

- include required basics like name, age, location, and representation
- avoid sounding robotic or over-rehearsed
- use upbeat conversational energy
- include a fun fact or personality detail
- end warmly and sincerely

This is direct source material for future lessons on slates, personality, self-tape presence, and audition presentation.

### Financial Planning for Young Actors

Core topics:

- financial education
- income tracking
- tax awareness
- budgeting
- separating professional and personal expenses
- emergency funds
- long-term savings
- education and retirement planning
- insurance
- trusts and estate planning
- working with financial advisors and legal professionals

This section is strong source material for parent-operations, career-cost, and financial-planning lessons.

### Audition Tracker

This section makes the case for tracking:

- auditions
- expenses
- progress over time
- casting director history
- career data

It supports future lessons on organization, workflow, and data-informed career planning.
