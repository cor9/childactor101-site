# Site Architecture

The Child Actor 101 Platform is organized around the journey parents take while navigating the entertainment industry.

Navigation should reflect user goals—not internal company structure or product categories.

Every page should answer one question:

**"What should I do next?"**

---

# Purpose

This document defines the information architecture, navigation philosophy, user journeys, and page relationships across the Child Actor 101 ecosystem.

It exists to ensure the platform grows intentionally instead of accumulating disconnected pages over time.

---

# Navigation Philosophy

Navigation should reduce decision fatigue.

Parents should never feel overwhelmed by dozens of choices.

Instead of asking visitors to understand the Child Actor 101 ecosystem, the website should guide them naturally through it.

The platform should feel like moving through chapters of a book rather than wandering a collection of unrelated websites.

---

# Growth Strategy

New pages should strengthen existing pathways.

Do not create isolated destinations.

Every new page should answer:

Where did this visitor come from?

What question are they trying to answer?

What should they do next?

What related resources would genuinely help them?

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Child Actor 101 Platform should eventually become a unified educational ecosystem where visitors experience one consistent brand regardless of whether they are reading an article, using a tool, listening to the podcast, or enrolling in training.

Technology may evolve.

The user experience should remain seamless.
