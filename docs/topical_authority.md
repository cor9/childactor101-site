# Topical Authority Strategy

Child Actor 101 is building the most comprehensive educational resource for families navigating professional youth acting.

Our goal is not simply to rank for keywords.

Our goal is to become the most trusted authority on every major topic parents encounter throughout their child's acting journey.

This document defines those topics and how they connect throughout the Child Actor 101 ecosystem.

---

# Purpose

This document serves as the master editorial roadmap for:

- Website content
- Blog articles
- Podcast episodes
- YouTube videos
- Newsletter content
- Free guides
- Courses
- Lead magnets
- SEO planning
- Internal linking

Every new piece of content should strengthen an existing topic cluster or establish a new strategic cluster.

---

# Authority Philosophy

We do not chase trends.

We build comprehensive educational resources.

The platform should resemble a continually expanding textbook rather than a collection of blog posts.

Every major topic should eventually become the definitive online resource.

Every major topic should eventually contain:

# Internal Linking Philosophy

Every article should naturally connect readers to additional educational resources.

A typical path may look like:

Article

↓

Related Lesson

↓

Podcast Episode

↓

Free Guide

↓

Tool

↓

Training

↓

Newsletter

Links should always improve the reader's experience.

Never link solely for SEO.

---

# Content Lifecycle

Every cornerstone guide should be reviewed regularly.

Review for:

- Accuracy
- Industry updates
- New products
- New podcast episodes
- Better illustrations
- Additional internal links
- Updated statistics
- Fresh examples

Evergreen content should improve continuously.

---

# Measuring Topical Authority

Success is measured by:

- Complete topic coverage
- Search visibility
- Internal link depth
- Newsletter growth
- Podcast engagement
- Time on page
- Return visitors
- Brand searches
- Parent trust

Topical authority is earned through consistency, depth, and usefulness—not volume.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Child Actor 101 Platform should become the definitive online textbook for families pursuing professional youth acting.

A parent should be able to arrive knowing nothing about the industry and, through interconnected lessons, guides, podcasts, tools, and training, confidently navigate every stage of their child's career.

Every article, lesson, and resource should contribute to that single mission.
